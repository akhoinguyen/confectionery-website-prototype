About Us

Established in 1895, Coco's Chocolatiers is one of the most recognized chocolatiers in Canada. At Coco's, we strive to make everything top-quality: from our ingredients, our equipments to our products and services. It's the result of our century-old passion for chocolate making.

Our ingredients are carefully selected from around the world. Each and every one of them goes through our top-of-the-industry process to produce the finest chocolate. They are then beautifully packaged and delivered to our valued customers.

We believe chocolate making is a form of art, and with our passion and professionalism we hope to bring you the best experience you can have with chocolate.

All of our products are crafted using environmental-friendly materials, including our packaging. As a company, it is our duty to not just care about our customers but also to protect the environment. We truly believe in a sustainable future and we are taking steps to help the planet as much as we can.
